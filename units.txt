Quantities
==========

dens* - Density                         - 1/fm^3
xm*   - Effective mass                  - MeV
mu*   - Chemical potential              - MeV
nu*   - Effective chemical potential    - MeV
E*    - Energy density                  - MeV/fm^3
P*    - Pressure                        - MeV/fm^3
S*    - Entropy density                 - 1/fm^3
U*    - E* from thermodynamic relations - MeV/fm^3
chi   - Quark phase volume fraction     - Dimensionless
T     - Temperature                     - MeV

Particles
=========
u - Up quark
d - Down quark
s - Strange quark
p - Proton
n - Neutron
e - Electron
m - Muon
v - (Electron) neutrino
